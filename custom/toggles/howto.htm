The Battery Icon Creator

What are we doing here ?
########################################################################################################
This is the feature to add toggles to your flashable-zip.
You will find here some predefined Toggle-IconSets, but you can add your own IconSets as well!

HowTo Add your own Toggle-IconSets!

  • Goto the folder where you extracted the Rom Fumbler
  • Find the subfolder ./toggles/
  • See the predefined IconSets in there ?
  • Create a new subfolder(s) next to the predefined IconSets. For example "MySuperDuperToggles"
  • and place your custom toogle png's in that new folder.
  • That is all…after a restart of the Rom Fumbler you should see your IconSet in the dropdown box.
  • Hint: The Rom Fumbler will create an overview-png automaticly in your new subfolder on restart (if you want to show of your creations ?)
  • Hint: So if you want to stay flexible it might be a good idea to put xhdpi icons in here, even if you are on a hdpi ROM. (icons are resized to fit your Rom-Presets!)

When you create your flashable-zip…

  • The Icons will not be renamed! (So make sure Filenames match your Rom!!!)
  • The icons will be resized to the defined toggle-height (find it in Rom Settings)!
  • The resized icons will be deployed to a subfolder ./png/deploy/toggles/-IconSet- (in case you want to look at the resized icons)
  • The flashable-zip will be created!
########################################################################################################
The 2 predefined Toggles in here "(AOKP)..." are for AOKP-Roms only! Stock Roms have different filenames.
Don't flash these to Stock Roms !!!!
