This is the Lockring Feature

Her you can change/mod your lockring ... just choose one from the dropdownbox above.

In the RomSettings-Tab you find naming patterns for the lockring and the size (height) of it.

Learn how to find the right Rom Settings

How to add your own custom Lockrings

  • Search for the subfolder ./custom/lockhandles/...
  • See all the icons in there...just add your own designs/icons to this folder and restart the Rom-Fumbler.
  • You can now select your icons in the dropdownbox.
