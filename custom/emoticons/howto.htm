Emoticon Icon-Sets

This is the right place, if you have custom emoticon icons from somewhere ( downloaded from the internet, self-designed...) and want to add them to your flashable-zip!
You will find some predefined IconSets here, but you can add your own IconSets as well!
The predefined Icon-Sets are for AOKP Rom's and will probably not work on Stock Rom's!

Attention: This feature will flash/morph the Emoticons to framework-res.apk as default....
If you want to flash the icons to Mms.apk...select checkbox "Morph to Mms.apk" in Rom Settings!

Make sure you have the correct Rom Settings before you create a flashable-zip!

In framework-res.apk there are normally 17 emo*-icons
in Mms.apk there are normally 21 emo*-icons

Learn how to find the correct Rom Settings for your ROM

HowTo add your own IconSets:

  • Goto the folder where you extracted the "Rom Fumbler"
  • Find the subfolder ./custom/emoticons/
  • See the predefined IconSets in there ?
  • Create a new subfolder(s) next to the predefined IconSets. For example "CoolEmoticons"
  • and place your custom png's in that new folder.
  • That is all…after a restart of the Rom Fumbler you should see your IconSet in the dropdown box.
  • Hint: The Rom Fumbler will create an overview-png automaticly in your new subfolder on restart
    (if you want to show of your creations ?)
  • Hint: So if you want to stay flexible it might be a good idea to put xhdpi icons in here, even if you are on a hdpi ROM.
    (icons are resized to fit your ROM-Presets!)
  • Hint: If you create your own IconSet, it might be a good idea to start with the signal & wifi icons you find in your framework-res.apk.
    So you are sure you have all icons your ROM supports. And you have the correct filenames. Now replace these icons with your own designs.

When you create your flashable-zip…

  • The Icons will not be renamed!
    (So make sure filenames match your ROM!!!)
    In framework-res.apk there are normally 17 emo*-icons
    in Mms.apk there are normally 21 emo*-icons!!!
    So make sure to flash teh correct icon-set to the correct apk!!!!
  • The icons will be resized to the defined emoticon-height
    (find it in Rom Settings)!
  • The resized icons will be deployed to a subfolder ./png/deploy/Emoticons/<IconSetName>
    (in case you want to look at the resized icons, or use them for something else)
  • Finally the flashable-zip will be created!
    (Find it in ./flashablezip-out/...)
