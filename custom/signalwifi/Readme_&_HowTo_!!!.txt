########################################################################################################
What are we doing here ?
########################################################################################################
This is the folder where you can put your own sets of wifi & signal icons.
Create subfolder(s) in here for example "MySuperDuperWifiIcons" and place your wifi & signal png's in that folder.

The directory structure should look like this:

./custom/signalwifi/
           -->   (AOKP) xyz
           -->   (AOKP) sdfgg
           -->   MySuperDuperWifiIcons
           -->   ...
           
After that restart the "Battery Icon Creator". 
Hopefully it has found your new Folder "MySuperDuperWifiIcons" and will present it in the Signal$Wifi dropdownbox.

The icons are resized if you create a flashable-zip to the same height as battery icons!  (So they match the height of the batteries!)
So if you want to stay flexible it might be a good idea to put xhdpi icons in here, even if you are on a hdpi ROM. 
(As said...the icons are resized to fit your Rom-Presets!)


########################################################################################################
Attention !!!
########################################################################################################
The 2 predefined Icons in here "(AOKP)..." are for AOKP-Roms only! Stock Roms have different filenames. 
Don't flash these to Stock Roms !!!!
